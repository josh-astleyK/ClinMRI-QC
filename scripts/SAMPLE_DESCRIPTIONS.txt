Sample 2 - two-valued region (the key test for the mask confusion).
  Volume: 40x40x40. Inside the 20x20x20 mask, half the slices = 50,
          the other half = 150. Outside the mask = 0.
  GROUND TRUTH (stats over the mask, of the IMAGE not the mask):
    intensity_mean = (50+150)/2 = 100   <-- NOT 1 (the mask value)

    centroid_offset ~ 2.5 mm  (the brighter 150 half pulls the
          intensity-weighted centre of mass toward it -- so the
          centroid is NOT the geometric centre; this is expected.)
